Conveniently located inside the Beltway and with effortless access to local universities and research parks, Riverdale Park Station is a connected, thriving, and comfortable place to call home. Walkable to the Metro Green Line and with a private shuttle system to and from the University of Maryland College Park campus, you’ll never be too far from home. Whether you’re looking for Riverdale Park, MD apartments or homes near College Park, MD, you’ll find a lot to love in Riverdale Park Station.

Riverdale Park Station features 875 apartments and more than 100 townhomes — don’t miss this opportunity to call Riverdale Park home.
